## 👤 About Me

I am a **Data** Analyst with a strong foundation in SQL, Python, Power BI, and Excel, focused on building data-driven solutions that support key business decisions.  
I enjoy translating raw data into meaningful stories through visualizations, dashboards, and actionable insights.

I focus on:
- Data cleaning and transformation for reliable analysis  
- Exploratory data analysis (EDA) to uncover trends and patterns  
- Dashboard creation and reporting for stakeholders  
- Business insight generation to support strategic decisions
